# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

**Version:** 2.1
**Project:** ARBITRAGE-PRO
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-15

---

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Being respectful** — Disagreeing is fine, disrespect is not
- **Being collaborative** — We succeed together
- **Being constructive** — Critique ideas, not people
- **Being inclusive** — Welcome newcomers, mentor the inexperienced
- **Being professional** — High standards for everyone
- **Being empathetic** — Assume good intent

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- **Harassment** — Offensive comments, personal attacks, trolling
- **Discrimination** — Bias based on protected characteristics
- **Intimidation** — Threats, stalking, unwanted attention
- **Inappropriate content** — Sexual, violent, or offensive material
- **Disruption** — Sustained derailing, excessive self-promotion
- **Privacy violations** — Doxxing, sharing private information

---

## Our Responsibilities

### Maintainers

- Clarify standards of acceptable behavior
- Take appropriate corrective action for violations
- Remove, edit, or reject contributions that don't align with this Code
- Communicate decisions transparently

### Contributors

- Follow this Code in all project spaces
- Report violations to maintainers
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on the project's success

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories and issues
- All communication channels (Discord, forums, etc.)
- All project events (online and offline)
- All roles (maintainer, contributor, user)

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Report violations to: **conduct@arbitrage-pro.com**

Reports are confidential. Include:
- Description of behavior
- Date, time, and location
- Screenshots or evidence
- Any additional context

### Review Process

1. Report received and acknowledged
2. Maintainers review within 48 hours
3. Decision made by consensus (minimum 2 maintainers)
4. Action taken within 7 days
5. Reporter informed of outcome

### Consequences

| Violation | Consequence |
|---|---|
| Warning | Verbal or written warning |
| Temporary ban | 7-30 day exclusion from project |
| Permanent ban | Permanent exclusion from project |

Consequences are proportional to severity and history.
